About Las VegASH

Started by the late JC Simon, Las VegASH was his way of giving back to the amazing cigar community in Las Vegas. His vision was to have a place to share information about upcoming events, pictures and his thoughts on cigars. Sadly we lost JC in 2015, but his vision will live on.
